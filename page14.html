Disclaimer: This method is not guaranteed to work for everyone and may violate Instagram's terms of service. Use at your own risk.

Clear Instagram App Data

Go to your phone settings, find Instagram in the app list, and clear all app data and cache. This will require you to log in again.

Set a Human Profile Picture

Change your profile picture to a clear photo of a real person. This helps make your account appear more legitimate to Instagram's systems.

Post Three Photos with Location

Upload three photos to your Instagram account and make sure to add a location tag set to India for each post.

Disable Instagram Briefly

Turn off the Instagram app completely for about 1 minute. You can do this by force stopping the app or restarting your phone.
