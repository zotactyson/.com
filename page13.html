BROADCAST ENABLE METHOD

BY :~ zotactyson

Prerequisites

Before attempting to enable the broadcast feature, ensure your account meets these requirements:

Professional Account

Convert your account to a Professional/Business account

10K+ Followers

Your account should have at least 10,000 followers

Engaging Content

Have 1-2 posts with 10K+ likes and views

Note: After completing these prerequisites, your account will be ready for the broadcast feature.

Steps to Enable Broadcast

Join Beta Program

Go to Play Store and search for Instagram. Look for the option to join the Beta tester program and click the "Join" button. Wait for 4-5 minutes for the process to complete.

Clear App Data

Clear all data of the Instagram app from your device settings.

Login to Your Account

Log in to your 10K+ followers account after clearing the data.

Regular Usage

Use your account daily for the next 15 days to establish activity patterns.

Check for Updates

Check the Play Store daily for Instagram beta updates (they typically update 2-3 times daily).

Contact Instagram Support

Send an email to android-support@instagram.com with the subject: "Re: BROADCAST OPTION NOT ENABLED"

Email Template

Use this exact template when contacting Instagram support:

Dear Meta Team, I want to make a broadcast channel. The main motive of making broadcast is to create social awareness among people by spreading motivating slogans. There is a problem with direct messages which is taking more time, so I want to make a broadcast channel where people can join and can see those slogans. I have 100k+ followers but I don't get any broadcast option. Please give me broadcast access. Thank you for your assistance.
